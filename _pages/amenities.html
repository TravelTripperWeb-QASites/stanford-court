We’re obsessed with giving you the sweetest experience possible, and for us, that means rounding up the best of SF and making it available to you. Influenced by the progressive tech culture on which San Francisco and Silicon Valley thrive, Stanford Court hotel amenitieswere thoughtfully developed to keep you efficient and feeling your best while on the road.

Connect with our front desk and concierge team. We’re happy to help around the clock. The lobby and business center are equipped with MacBooks, iPads, and iPad minis, all running on high speed WiFi. Wirelessly print from any of our devices or your own.

Designed to both invigorate and relax mind and body, our 24-hour fitness center, bike share, and wellness program provide a game-changing experience.

Stanford Court San Francisco is also home to Commons café and the Nob Hill outpost of Seven Stills, sourcing food, coffee, and spirits from local farms and vendors.
